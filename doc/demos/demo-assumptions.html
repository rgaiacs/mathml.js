Demo for assumptions

MathML define three invisible operators:

  • &ApplyFunction;,
  • &InvisibleTimes;,
  • &InvisibleComma;.
And MathML.js use it to make assumptions.

Functions

A function of x named f, i.e. f ⁡ x , MUST be write as
<math> <mrow> <mi> f </mi> <mo> &#x2061; </mo> <mfenced> <mi> x </mi> </mfenced> </mrow> </math>

And the sine function, i.e. sin ⁡ x , MUST be write as
<math> <mrow> <mi> sin </mi> <mo> &#x2061; </mo> <mfenced> <mi> x </mi> </mfenced> </mrow> </math>

Product

The product of two variables, x and y, i.e. x ⁢ y , MUST be write as
<math> <mrow> <mi> x </mi> <mo> &#x2062; </mo> <mi> y </mi> </mrow> </math>

Dimensions

In the case of a variable that have at least two dimensions, i.e. a x ⁣ y , MUST be write as
<math> <msub> <mi> x </mi> <mrow> <mi> x </mi> <mo> &#x2063; </mo> <mi> y </mi> </mrow> </msub> </math>
